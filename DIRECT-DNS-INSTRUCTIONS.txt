RELIABLEBOSTON.COM - DIRECT DNS SETUP  
NO PROXY / NO CLOUDFLARE ROUTING
========================================

STEP 1: DEPLOY TO CLOUDFLARE PAGES
1. Go to: https://dash.cloudflare.com
2. Click "Pages" → "Create a project" → "Upload assets"
3. Name: reliableboston-com  
4. Upload: reliableboston-website.zip file
5. Click "Deploy site"

STEP 2: GET YOUR PAGES IP ADDRESS
After deployment:
1. Go to your Pages project
2. Click "Custom domains" tab
3. The IP address will be shown when you add your domain

STEP 3: ADD DNS RECORDS AT YOUR DOMAIN REGISTRAR
Log into where you bought reliableboston.com and add:

Record Type: A
Name: @
Value: [IP from Pages project]  
TTL: 300

Record Type: CNAME
Name: www
Value: reliableboston-com.pages.dev
TTL: 300

STEP 4: CONNECT DOMAIN IN CLOUDFLARE PAGES
1. In your Pages project → Custom domains  
2. Add "reliableboston.com"
3. Add "www.reliableboston.com"
4. IMPORTANT: Turn OFF proxy (gray cloud, not orange)

STEP 5: WAIT & TEST
- DNS propagation: 5-30 minutes
- SSL certificate: 5-15 minutes  
- Test: https://reliableboston.com
